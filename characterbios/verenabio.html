Verena Character Bio

Character Chart

  • Verena Fletcher
  • Verena means "protector," Fletcher means "maker of arrows"
  • Ammon started calling her "Vee" when they were little and it stuck around even after they all grew up
  • Born October 18, 1497
  • 26 years old (at the start of story)

Physical Appearance

  • 5'5
  • Chocolate brown hair
  • Hair kept in a braid
  • Emerald green eyes
  • Tan skin tone
  • No scars or special markings
  • Looks like a perfect mix of her parents

Personality

  • Can usually keep calm during intense situations
  • Isn't necessarily shy but is quieter around new people than she is around family and friends
  • Loves her family
  • Usually gives people the benefit of the doubt but will hold a grudge once they pass the limits of her good graces
  • Takes the safety of her loved ones very seriously
