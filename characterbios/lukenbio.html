Luken Character Bio

Character Chart

  • Luken Shaw
  • Luken means "light," Shaw means "dweller by the woods"
  • Dainan calls him "sunshine"
  • Born September 13, 1519
  • 4 years old (at the start of story)

Physical Appearance

  • ~3'4
  • Honey blode hair with random strands of blue scattered throughout it
  • Hair kept down, very fluffy
  • Sky blue eyes
  • Tan skin tone
  • has scattered patches of scales on his body, the ones dotted on his cheeks are reminiscent of Linn's freckles
  • Has a tail and fangs
  • Has floppy, pointed ears like Dainan, they're blue at the tips

Personality

  • Sweet bean child
  • Very hyper and playful
  • Loves his family
  • Is young and doesn't always understand when he's supposed to calm down or be quiet
  • Is curious and likes showing his random findings to anyone around
